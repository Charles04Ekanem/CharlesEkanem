Agriculture Datasets — Akwa Ibom (Southern Nigeria)

About this dataset

This dataset folder contains curated images collected from the Akwa Ibom Southern region of Nigeria. It includes photographs of crops, vegetables, and animals commonly found in the region. Use cases: classification, transfer learning, agricultural monitoring research, and educational projects.

Modalities

Images (JPEG/PNG), organized in class-labeled folders.

  • Organization: class-labeled folders (train/val) — recommended 70/30 split
  • Format: JPEG/PNG images, suggested resolution <= 1024x1024
